PAE Accepted at ICML 2025

Our paper on Proposer-Agent-Evaluator (PAE) for autonomous skill discovery in foundation model internet agents has been accepted at ICML 2025. PAE introduces a framework where agents autonomously propose, attempt, and evaluate new skills in open-ended web environments.

PrefEval Accepted at ICLR 2025 as Oral

Our work on evaluating personalized preference following in LLMs has been accepted as an oral presentation at ICLR 2025. The paper examines how well LLMs can recognize and follow user preferences in a personalized manner.
